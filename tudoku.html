What is Tudoku

Tudoku is a game made by mearging tetris and sudoku.

How to play Tudoku

The game contains a 9*9 gride.

The Present number block is shown below

Clicking on a cell on the gride will place the Present number block on the column of the cell you clicked

If a row,column or a 3*3 grid of cell's contains all the numbers from 1 to 9 then it get deleted

The goal of the game is to not fill the whole grid
